Intelligence Computing Department wins it all.

On May 27, 2025, Dong-Eui University’s International Exchange Team brought the campus to life with an exciting football event for the English Track department. The tournament saw enthusiastic participation from students in both the Global Hospitality Business Administration and Intelligence Computing departments, showcasing teamwork, sportsmanship, and school pride.

As the tournament progressed through the preliminary and semifinal rounds, the competition grew more intense. In the end, both finalist teams came from the same department—Intelligence Computing—with second and third semester students facing off in a thrilling final match.

The atmosphere was electric as professors and students gathered around the field, cheering loudly and showing their support for both teams. It wasn’t just a game—it was a celebration of community and connection across the English Track.

After a close and exciting match, the second semester team emerged victorious, claiming the title of 2025 Spring Semester soccer champions. The event not only crowned winners but also strengthened bonds among students and faculty, making it a memorable day for everyone involved.
