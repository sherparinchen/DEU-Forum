How to Get an ARC (Alien Registration Card) in Busan

All international students staying in South Korea for more than 90 days are required to apply for an Alien Registration Card (ARC). In Busan, the process starts by booking an appointment online through the HiKorea website. Once scheduled, students must visit the Busan Immigration Office with the necessary documents including passport, application form, visa certificate, and a passport-sized photo.

During your visit, you’ll submit your documents and pay a processing fee of 30,000 KRW. The process usually takes around 3–4 weeks, after which you will receive a notification to pick up your ARC. It’s crucial to apply within 90 days of your arrival in Korea to avoid penalties or visa complications.

The ARC serves as your official ID in Korea, allowing you to open a bank account, register for phone services, and travel domestically. For more details and appointment booking, visit the official HiKorea portal at https://www.hikorea.go.kr.
