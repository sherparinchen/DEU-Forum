My Experience on ICC 2025 at Dong Eui University

Recently, my team and I participated in the ICC 2025 coding competition held at Dong-Eui University. Alongside my talented teammates Azhar and Rafi, we competed against about 12 other teams in what was an intense and exciting event.

Professor Hamdi was present to oversee the competition, adding to the thrill and prestige of the event. We chose Python as our programming language, which helped us tackle the challenges efficiently and effectively.

The competition was not only fun but also a great learning experience. We felt the pressure but also the joy of working together and solving problems under time constraints. Ultimately, we were thrilled to win first place and the prize of 100,000 KRW!

Participating in ICC 2025 was a highlight of my time at Dong-Eui University. It boosted my confidence and expanded my coding skills while creating lasting memories with my teammates. I encourage everyone to take part in such competitions—they are invaluable opportunities for growth and fun.
