GIC opens their new website

In May 2025, Global Intelligence Computing at Dong-Eui University officially launched its new website, marking a significant step toward enhancing its digital presence and accessibility. The new platform is designed to better serve students, faculty, researchers, and international partners with updated content and streamlined navigation.

The website features comprehensive information about the department’s academic programs, research initiatives, faculty profiles, and global collaboration efforts. It also includes announcements, event updates, and resources to support both current and prospective students interested in the field of intelligent computing.

This modernized web portal reflects the department’s commitment to innovation and transparency. Visitors can explore the new website at https://gic.deu.ac.kr/gic/index.do to learn more about Global Intelligence Computing at Dong-Eui University.
